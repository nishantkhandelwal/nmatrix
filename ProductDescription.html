NMatrix Connector supports these integration touchpoints on e-commerce marketplaces:

  • Products: Easily access and manipulate your catalogs, publish all your products to different marketplaces at once.

  • Inventory: Get stock updates and manage all marketplace inventory levels.

  • Customer: Manage customer data including contacts and previous orders.

  • Orders: Seamlessly receive, process, and manage orders across all storefronts.

  • Shipping and fulfillment: Manage order fulfilments and show customize shipping rates.

Integrate Nmatrix Connector Into Your Ecommerce Store With 6 Simple Steps

1. Get started in Minutes Using These Simple Steps

You start by installing your webstore using Nmatrix connector, follow the stepwise processes, and get started in no time.

2. Quickly Upload Your Products With Ease

To make all your product information available on your webstore (whether Shopify, Lazada, JD, or Shopee) you have to upload all existing product information from Microsoft dynamic 365 business central into your online store.

3. Customize Your Ecommerce Store To Your Preferences

Get your online storefront looking the way you want it with customizable options and features that let you manage your store from your Microsoft dynamic 365 business central. Add pictures, product descriptions, and change prices of products across all your marketplaces at once.

4. Hassle-free Order Processing

With your Nmatrix connector, all orders from your different online marketplaces will be received into one central place-your Microsoft dynamic business central. This makes it easy to sort through orders and ship accordingly.

5. Quickly access payments

Nmatrix connector makes it easy for you to access all your payment from different e-commerce marketplace in one Microsoft dynamic 365 business central database. The payment processor you choose to use in your store does not matter.

6. Updates and Order Confirmation

As soon as you update an order as “shipped” our systems will automatically send a notification to your customer along with order shipping and tracking information.
