About Us


Who We Are :

We are NMatrix, a Singapore-based company committed to transforming modern-day businesses with innovative marketplace management solutions that amplify business growth. We aim to help e-commerce businesses operate more efficiently and optimize their processes. Our product- NMatrix Connector is a next-generation connector between marketplace stores and Microsoft dynamic business central allowing for seamless data exchange and operation management.


Our Mission :

Our focus is the same as always, to continually develop smart systems for businesses to thrive and to create simple automation that improves efficiency. We believe that reducing manual processes and leveraging automation will decrease downtime and empower businesses for growth. For this reason, we will strive to make our system good enough to get the job done.


Our platform :

We have developed a purpose-built platform with scalable integrations and connectivity for business central. NMatrix Connector is designed to meet the needs of today's e-commerce businesses. With well-defined features and integrations, it is a simple tool that is transforming the way enterprises run their business. We will continue to adapt our product to meet current technological advances so that businesses can experience significant growth.


Our Team :

We are a professional team of ERP system specialists with a combined solid experience of over 25 years between us. We are passionate about working on ERP systems and integration projects that add value to users and we have worked on SME implementation ERP solutions as well as retail integration solutions that are making a huge difference today. We will keep combining our technical knowledge, experience, and creative approach to create new solutions and improve upon already existing ones.
